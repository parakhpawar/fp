EMERGENCY TIPS
🚨 Stay calm and assess the situation.
📞 Call emergency services immediately if needed.
🏃‍♂️ Evacuate the area if it's unsafe.
🛡️ Follow instructions from authorities.
👥 Help others if it's safe to do so.
📱 Keep your phone charged and have emergency contacts ready.
🚗 If driving, pull over safely and turn on hazard lights.
💧 In case of flood, move to higher ground.
🔥 For fire, stop, drop, and roll if clothes are on fire.
🚑 Know basic first aid and CPR.
